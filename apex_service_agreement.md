## 2. TERMS & LEGAL CONDITIONS

### 2.1 Payment Schedule & Billing
1. **Subscription Accounts:** Subscription fees are billed automatically every 30 days starting on the day the project kicks off. A minimum contract term of 12 months is required.
2. **Upfront Accounts:** A non-refundable deposit of **50% of the total Setup Fee** is due upon signing this Agreement to reserve scheduling. The remaining 50% balance is due immediately upon website completion and prior to launching the site live to the public.
3. **Overdue Payments:** Payments not received within 15 days of the invoice date will incur a late fee of **1.5% per month** (18% per annum) on the outstanding balance.

### 2.2 Client-Caused Delays
The Client agrees to provide all text, copy, product data, and branding images requested by Apex Web Studio in a timely manner. 1. **Included Maintenance (Opt-In / Subscriptions):** Includes up to 3 hours of minor content edits (such as text adjustments, image swaps, or simple link updates) per billing cycle. Unused hours do not roll over to subsequent months.
