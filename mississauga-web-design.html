QuestionsWeb Design in Mississauga — FAQ

Common questions from Mississauga business owners about working with Apex Web Studio.

Do you build websites for businesses based in Mississauga?

Yes — Mississauga is our home base. Our studio is located in Mississauga, ON, and a large share of our clients are local businesses across Square One, Port Credit, Streetsville, Erin Mills and the surrounding areas.

How much does a website cost for a Mississauga small business?

Our subscription plans start at $149 CAD/mo with $0 upfront on a 12-month term, or you can pay a one-time build fee starting at $499 CAD. Pricing is the same whether you’re in Mississauga or anywhere else in the GTA — it depends on the number of pages and features, not your location.

Can we meet in person to discuss my project?

Absolutely. Because we’re based in Mississauga we can meet local clients in person when it’s helpful, though most of our clients prefer a quick call and everything handled online.

Will my website help me show up on Google for Mississauga searches?

Yes. Every build includes local SEO foundations — proper schema, heading structure and Google Business Profile alignment — so you have the on-page groundwork to rank for ‘near me’ and ‘in Mississauga’ searches. Our Local SEO & Map Pack add-on takes it further with citations and Google Maps 3-Pack optimisation.

How long does it take to launch a Mississauga business website?

Most Landing and Growth sites launch within 1–2 weeks of receiving your content. Larger Enterprise and E-commerce builds take a little longer depending on scope.
