Our Heritage The Standard of Custom Woodwork

Since 1997, Lakeview Millwork Ltd. has been Mississauga's premier manufacturer of custom commercial and high-end residential millwork. Our dedication to woodwork extends from complex architectural paneling to tailor-made modern corporate interiors.

We blend time-honored woodworking techniques with state-of-the-art 3D modeling and precision CNC machining. As proud members of the Architectural Woodwork Manufacturers Association of Canada (AWMAC), our products guarantee premium standards of durability and aesthetics.

  • CNC Precision Machining & Fitting
  • Premium-grade Sustainable Hardwoods
  • Complete Design, Production, & Installation
  • Award-Winning Hospitality & Retail Builds
