QuestionsWeb Design in Toronto — FAQ

Common questions from Toronto business owners about working with Apex Web Studio.

Do you work with small businesses in Toronto?

Yes. We’re a GTA-based studio (headquartered in nearby Mississauga) and we build websites for small businesses right across Toronto — downtown, midtown, Scarborough, North York and Etobicoke.

How much does web design cost in Toronto?

Plans start at $149 CAD/mo with $0 upfront, or a one-time build from $499 CAD. Our pricing is transparent and fixed by scope — you’ll never pay a ‘Toronto premium’ that big downtown agencies charge.

What makes you different from other Toronto web design agencies?

Most agencies build on WordPress themes or page builders that load slowly and bloat over time. We hand-code every site from scratch for speed, security and clean structure — and we bundle hosting, maintenance and updates into one flat monthly plan.

Can you help my Toronto business rank on Google?

Yes. Every website ships with the on-page SEO foundations Google needs — schema markup, proper headings, fast performance and mobile optimisation. Add the Local SEO & Map Pack to actively pursue Google Maps 3-Pack placement for your Toronto searches.

Do we need to meet in person?

No — most Toronto clients prefer a quick call and everything handled online, which keeps your project moving fast. If you’d like to meet, we’re a short drive away in Mississauga.
